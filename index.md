# Privacy Policy for NativeKid

**Last Updated:** November 27, 2024

## Introduction

NativeKid ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- **Name**: Your display name from your Google account
- **Email Address**: Your email address from your Google account  
- **Profile Picture**: Your profile photo from your Google account
- **User ID**: A unique identifier from Google

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent in app
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data if the app crashes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide App Functionality**: Enable sign-in and save learning progress
- **Personalize Experience**: Display your name and photo in the app
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Provide Support**: Respond to your questions and technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase Authentication**: User authentication data is stored securely by Google Firebase
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
- **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

- **Purpose**: User authentication and data storage
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google Sign-In
- **Purpose**: User authentication
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

NativeKid is designed for children ages 3-8. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent
- Google Sign-In requires parental supervision for children under 13
- We do not use children's data for advertising or marketing purposes
- Parents can review and delete their child's data at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

### Delete Your Data
Request deletion of your account and all associated data by:
- Emailing us at: **triplemzim@gmail.com**
- We will delete your data within 30 days of your request

### Opt-Out
You can use the app as a "Guest" without signing in, though progress will not be saved.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for 2 years may be automatically deleted
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Cookies and Tracking

NativeKid does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies for advertising purposes.
